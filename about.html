ABOUT

We are a small nail bar with big dreams. We started the place when we realised Bangalore really needs a bite out of the nail bar trends from all over the world.

Our aim is to run a fun place where you can come get your nails and hair done before a party, or your routine manicure and pedicure, have a fun day out with the girls and even if you want to bring your little one in for nice mommy and daughter time.

Keep an eye out for our on going offers and membership deals, and looking to see you at Nailbox soon!

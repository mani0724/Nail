Brows & Lip Services

Book an Appointment

Brows Shape & Tint

This service includes professional eyebrow shaping followed by a tint to enhance your natural brow color. We sculpt your brows based on your facial structure and use a semi-permanent tint to fill sparse areas.

  • Defined, fuller-looking brows

  • Saves time on daily makeup

  • Lasts up to 2–3 weeks

  • Natural and polished look

Brow Lamination

Brow lamination is like a perm for your brows—lifting and setting the hairs in a uniform direction to create a fuller, feathered appearance. It’s perfect for taming unruly brows or achieving that sleek, brushed-up look.

  • Fuller, lifted brows

  • Smooths and sets hair direction

  • Lasts 6–8 weeks

  • Low-maintenance styling

Microblading

Microblading is a semi-permanent technique where fine hair-like strokes are drawn onto the skin using a pigment. Ideal for sparse or thinning brows, it gives a very natural, realistic finish.

  • Natural-looking brows

  • Long-lasting results (12–18 months)

  • Custom shape & color

  • Saves time on daily brow makeup

Microshading

Also known as “powder brows,” microshading uses a stippling technique to create a soft, powdered effect, similar to brow makeup. It’s ideal for people with oily skin or those looking for a more filled-in appearance.

  • Soft, shaded brow look

  • Gentle on sensitive skine

  • Lasts 1–2 years

  • Great for a makeup-like finish

Lip Blush

Lip blush is a semi-permanent cosmetic tattoo that enhances the natural color and shape of your lips. It adds a soft tint, improves symmetry, and gives your lips a more youthful, fuller appearance.

  • Natural tint and even tone

  • Enhances lip shape

  • Long-lasting color (up to 2 years)

  • Reduces need for daily lip color

Lip Colour Correction

Lip colour correction helps neutralize dark or uneven tones in the lips, especially for those with hyperpigmentation. This treatment evens out the color before adding your desired shade through lip blush

  • Corrects dark or patchy lips

  • Prepares lips for blush tattoo

  • Customized color matching

  • Boosts lip vibrancy and confidence
